COVID-19 Simulation Tool

by Ragshanan Ragunathan


This is a tool to simulate COVID-19 and its likelihood of spreading through a population. An individual can be Susceptible, Infectious, Recovered or Deceased. This simulation is closely modelled after the SIRD compartmental system in epidemiology. You can set all parameters manually using the sliders and then press the "Start" button or you can select the "Use current RKI data" checkbox to automatically set Transmission Rate, Recovery Rate, Mortality Rate and number of initial infectious individuals. You can change the Vaccination Rate, Immunity-Breakthrough Rate and Mobility Rate while the simulation is running.
















Lockdown: Press and hold to invoke a total lockdown.
Isolation: Hover to invoke social distancing of infectious individuals.
Event: Hover to throw an event.
